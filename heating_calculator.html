Calculation Formulas

Total heat requirement:
Heat Needed = Volume × 0.035 kWh/m³/day × (Desired Temp - 5°C) × Season Days
Propane:
Cost = (Heat Needed ÷ (Energy Density × Efficiency)) × Price + (Standing Charge × Season Days)
Energy density: 7.0 kWh/L, 25.3 kWh/m³, 13.9 kWh/kg
Kerosene:
Cost = (Heat Needed ÷ (10.0 kWh/L × Efficiency)) × Price
Electricity:
Cost = (Heat Needed ÷ Efficiency) × Price + (Standing Charge × Season Days)
Wood:
Cost = (Heat Needed ÷ (1600 kWh/tonne × Efficiency)) × Price
